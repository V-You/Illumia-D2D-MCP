Campus tour notes -- State University dining services:
- Main dining hall: 6 registers, meal plan swipe + credit card
- Student union food court: 3 vendors, each with own POS
- Library cafe: 1 register, lots of grab-and-go
- Students and staff use physical prox cards for meal plans and building access
- Separate reporting systems for each dining location -- no unified view
- IT mentioned they want Apple Wallet for student IDs
- Nursing school has subsidized meal program, billed manually via spreadsheets
- Recreation center vending: 4 machines, cash only
